Cheat Codes (& Other Helpful Links)

Science

  • ScienceDirect: Access to scientific, technical, and medical research articles and journals.

  • Science News: News articles, features, and analysis on the latest scientific research and discoveries.

  • PLOS ONE: Access to peer-reviewed research articles across a variety of scientific disciplines.

  • PubMed: Access to research articles and journals in the biomedical and life sciences.

Can't Miss Scholarships!

  • Society of Women Engineers Scholarships

    This organization offers a variety of scholarships for women pursuing degrees in engineering, computer science, and other STEM fields

  • Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship

    This scholarship is awarded to women studying computer science, technology, or a related field and includes a monetary award and an invitation to attend the annual Google Scholars' Retreat.

  • P.E.O. Program for Continuing Education

    This program provides need-based grants to women who are returning to school to complete a degree in a STEM field.

  • Microsoft Research Graduate Women's Scholarship

    This scholarship supports women pursuing a PhD in computer science, electrical engineering, or mathematics at a participating university.
