Cryptanalysis

Cryptoanalysis is the practice of analyzing and deciphering encrypted data, including messages, codes, and ciphers. It involves using various methods and tools to decode and interpret the hidden meaning of the encrypted data.

Skillset Requirements

  • Strong mathemicatical and analytical skill
  • Proficiency in programming
  • Attention to detail especially in figuring out patterns
