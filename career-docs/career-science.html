Interesting Careers in Science

Forensics

Forensics is the scientific application of techniques and principles to investigate crime scenes and provide evidence for legal proceedings. This field involves the use of a variety of disciplines, including biology, chemistry, physics, and computer science, to analyze physical evidence such as fingerprints, DNA, fibers, and gunshot residue, among others. Forensic experts use their specialized knowledge and training to identify and analyze evidence that can help solve crimes and bring justice to victims.

Skillset Requirements

  • Attention to detail
  • Critical thinking
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Strong communication skills

Potential Majors

  • Forensic Science
  • Criminal Justice
  • Chemistry
