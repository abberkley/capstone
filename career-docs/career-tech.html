Interesting Careers in Technology

Photogrammetry

Photogrammetry is the science of making measurements and creating 3D models from photographs. As a career, photogrammetry is used in a variety of industries, including surveying, mapping, construction, and entertainment. A photogrammetrist uses specialized software to analyze images and create accurate 3D models of landscapes, buildings, and other structures.

Skillset Requirements

  • Attention to detail
  • Technical proficiency
  • Analytical skills

Potential Majors

  • Geomatics
  • Computer Science
  • Remote Sensing
