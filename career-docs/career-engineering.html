Interesting Careers in Engineering

fashion engineer

A fashion engineer is a professional who combines their expertise in engineering and design to create innovative and functional clothing and accessories. They use their knowledge of materials, mechanics, and manufacturing processes to develop and improve the performance, quality, and sustainability of fashion products.

Skillset Requirements

  • Strong fundation in math and science
  • Knowledge of textiles
  • Proficiency in computer-aided design (CAD)

Potential Majors

  • Fashion Design and Technology
  • Textile Engineering
  • Industrial Engineering
