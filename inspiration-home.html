Fueling your fire

Discover inspiration and ignite your passion for STEM with our page dedicated to showcasing the stories of remarkable women who have broken barriers and made groundbreaking contributions in their fields. Let these stories serve as a catalyst for your own journey in STEM.

Technology

Elizabeth J. Fienler

Elizabeth Feinler was an internet pioneer who played a critical role in the development of the ARPANET, the precursor to the modern internet.

Mathematics

Katherine Johnson

Katherine Johnson was a brilliant mathematician and NASA trailblazer who played a vital role in the success of the early U.S. space program, including the Apollo missions.

Science

Rosalind Franklin

Rosalind Franklin was a pioneering chemist and X-ray crystallographer whose work was crucial to the discovery of the structure of DNA.
